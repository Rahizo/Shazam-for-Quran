# Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: [date]

## Overview

[Company/App Name] helps users identify possible Quran surah and ayah matches from short recitation recordings.

## Information We Process

When you use the recognition feature, we process:

- A short audio recording you submit.
- The generated transcript of that recording.
- Technical request information such as IP address, browser, device type, timestamps, and server logs.

## How We Use Information

We use submitted audio and transcripts to:

- Identify likely Quran surah and ayah matches.
- Improve reliability, security, abuse prevention, and debugging.

## Audio Retention

Raw audio uploads are processed server-side and deleted after the recognition request completes. We do not intentionally store raw recitation recordings as user history in the MVP.

## Service Providers

Depending on the recognition mode, audio or transcript data may be processed by:

- OpenAI, for speech-to-text transcription.
- DeepSeek, if transcript cleanup is enabled.
- Hosting and infrastructure providers used to run the website and API.

## Quran Data And Recitation Audio

The app displays Quran text, translations, metadata, and recitation audio from third-party sources. Their terms may also apply.

## Children

This app is not intended to collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us.
